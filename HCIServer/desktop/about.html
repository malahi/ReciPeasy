Welcome to Recip-Easy, where learning a new recipe is, well, easy!

In our website there are many different recipes for different types of courses. You can browse our collection and find your next dream meal. Recip-Easy was made so everyone can easily learn new recipes (possibly from scratch) and enjoy cooking more by expanding their cooking variety.

The website was created by Gilad Chen and Malah Izraev on the 29th of February, 2019. Beforehand, Malah had the great idea that is this website: using the simplicity of the internet to make cooking easier. Gilad then came up with the name. The two built Recip-Easy for 3 and a half years to make it what it is today.
